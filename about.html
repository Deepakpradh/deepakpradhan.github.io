My Teaching Philosophy

Visual First

Every complex concept can be visualized. I create animations and diagrams that make abstract physics tangible.

Concept Over Calculation

Understanding why formulas work is more important than memorizing them. I focus on building intuitive frameworks.

Problem-Solving Mindset

Physics is about solving puzzles. I train students to approach problems systematically and creatively.

The goal of physics education isn't just to pass exams—it's to develop a way of thinking that reveals the hidden patterns in our universe.
